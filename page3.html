About Us

Mission Statement

“Salem College, a women`s college grounded in the liberal arts, values its students as individuals, develops their leadership potential, and prepares them to create healthier and more equitable communities.”

Vision Statement

Salem College is the premier women`s college committed to advancing equity in health and in our society.

Facts & Statistics

  • Student-Faculty Ratio: 9:1
  • Degree Programs: 58
  • Geographic Distrubution: 27 States
  • Average Class Size: 9

Core Values

Intellectual Curiosity

We embrace a lifelong passion for learning and seek to explore ideas beyond the obvious.

Intentional Community

We foster meaningful connections that support growth, collaboration, and mutual respect.

Equity and Belonging

We ensure everyone feels valued, included, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives.

Innovative Spirit

We pursue creativity and new approaches to solve challenges and drive progress.

Courageous Leadership

We lead with boldness, taking responsibility and making principled decisions even in the face of difficulty.

Honor and Integrity

We act with honesty, fairness, and accountability in all our interactions.
